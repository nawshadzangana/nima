Our Story

More Than a Haircut

"Every man deserves to walk out feeling like the best version of himself."

Nima Haircut was born from a passion for craftsmanship and an obsession with detail. Located in the heart of Sulaymaniyah, we've built a space where precision meets personality — where every client is treated as an individual, not just another appointment.

We blend international barbering techniques with an understanding of local style culture, delivering results that are clean, modern, and uniquely yours. From sharp fades to sculpted beards, we take pride in every millimeter.
